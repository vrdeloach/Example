About Us

I was born in the Northeastern part of Thailand and have been cooking since I was 5 years old (as it a Thai custom that young girls must always learn how to prepare the traditional dishes of their region.) Our food is world-renowned for its spiciness and fullness of flavor (and of course, its very healthy ingredients.) We begin with the fresh ingredients such as vegetables and rice and then flavor them in a centuries-old Thai manner. I want to share this wonderful style of cooking with you and I am delighted provide American families the opportunity to experience the very best of traditional Thai cuisine. I hope you enjoy dining with us and we are happy to alter any dishes to your personal taste. We do not use MSG and, if requested, we will gladly reduce sodium or oil as much as possible without changing the flavor of your dish.
