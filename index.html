Welcome!

We are a small restaurant in Jackson, TN. serving authentic Thai cuisine!

We offer dine in and take out. We have private dining rooms avaliable, no need to make reservations.

All of our food is prepared fresh in our kitchen and made from organic ingredients. We offer many health-conscious food items and can adjust any menu item to your liking.

Bold Flavors, Memorable Meals

Here at Thai Cafe, we strive to provide you with deliciously authentic Thai cuisine. We pride ourselves in our delicious food and invite you to visit during our hours.

We offer everything from curry to shrimp and our meals are avaliable however spicy you want them.
